Super Nintendo

The Super Nintendo (SNES for short) is the main game console I grew up playing as a kid. I have many great memories with it, which is not surprising as it is know as one of the best retro consoles from the 1990's. Some of the highlights of the system are Super Mario World, The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past, and Final Fantasy 6 (released as FF 3 here in North America). These games are still highly playable today for their gameplay and story.
